Our Products

Our wholesome fruit bars from burst with flavors both familiar and new. Whether you remember mango just like your grandmother sliced it for you or got a bubblegum flavored bar just for fun, a Helados del Sol fruit bar means fun flavors for you and your family and your friends.

Our all-natural fruit bars use the finest fruits, vegetables, herbs and spices available.

From subtler flavors such as strawberry, which uses sliced strawberries instead of just pureed strawberries, to mamey, which contains a puree of the “tropical apricot”, our products use the best ingredients available to us.

Fundamentally, our products refresh you whether the weather is warm out or you just want to have a delicious, tropical flavor in your belly.

In addition to fruit bars, we also sell Boli’s, our version of the popular bolly’s sold across the United States from your neighborhood ice cream seller."
